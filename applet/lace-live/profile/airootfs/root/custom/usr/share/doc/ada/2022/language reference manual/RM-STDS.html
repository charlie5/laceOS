This is the draft version of the Ada Reference Manual with extra annotations explaining the reasons behind the rules. This is mainly intended for compiler implementers and language lawyers.

This is the third edition of the Ada Reference Manual; versions with and without the annotations are available here.

This document explains the enhancements of Ada 2012, and provides examples of their use.

This document explains the enhancements of Ada 2005, and provides examples of their use.

Ada Issues
Ada Issues are the working documents of the ARG, which is charged with maintaining the Ada standard. Look here for corrections issued after the publication of the Standard, along with discarded ideas and additional information on accepted proposals and corrections.
